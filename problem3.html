Problem 3 — DQD with Decoherence in an Aharonov-Bohm Circuit

Interactive model for DQD with decoherence in an Aharonov-Bohm circuit

Problem Assignment

Extend the Double Quantum Dot model to include decoherence within the D'Amato-Pastawski (non-Hermitian effective Hamiltonian) framework.

Consider an Aharonov-Bohm interferometer where orbitals A and B are independently coupled to environmental reservoirs (analogous to voltage probes).

You may assume:

  • VAB = VU = 1
  • εA = εB = εC = 0
  1. Analyze analytically the effect of decoherence on resonances and antiresonances.
  2. Produce numerical plots for different parameter regimes.
  3. Discuss the progressive suppression of quantum interference.
